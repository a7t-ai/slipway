## Persona

A few sentences in first person about who you are and what you build.

## Voice and tone

- Short and direct.
- Practical, not corporate.
- Specific over generic.

## Writing rules

- Never use em dashes or en dashes. Use commas, periods, or restructure the sentence.
- First-person, direct.
- App or product names follow your house style (all caps, title case, etc.).

### Anti-slop rules

These patterns are AI giveaways. Avoid them.

**Banned phrases:**
- "Delve into", "Unpack". Say "look at" or "break down".
- "This signals that", "This underscores". State your actual opinion.
- "Navigate the complexities of", "In an ever-changing landscape". Be specific.
- "Synergies", "Leverage", "Holistic approach". Say what worked and what you changed.
- "Powerful opportunity", "Foster a culture of", "Lean into". Corporate filler.

**Banned patterns:**
- Opening paragraphs with "Moreover", "Furthermore", "That said", "Additionally", "Importantly". Read it out loud first.
- "Bold: explanation" bullet points. Just write the point.
- Generic wrap-up conclusions. If the conclusion is not specific to this content, cut it.
- "Sounds smart, means nothing" paragraphs. If a paragraph cannot be summarized in one plain sentence, delete it.
- Forced negation framing ("not this but that"). State the thing directly.
- Staccato repetition ("not this, not this, not this, but that"). Pick one contrast.
- Excessive adverbs ("quietly underscores", "fundamentally shifts"). Say what happened.

## Content principles

- Maximum one emoji per piece of copy. Most pieces need none.
- Specific beats generic. Concrete beats abstract.
